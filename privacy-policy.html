Privacy Policy for Fast P2P Vnzla FREE

Last Updated: December 2024

Introduction

Fast P2P Vnzla FREE ("we", "our", or "the Extension") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our Chrome extension.

Information We Collect

Data Stored Locally

The Extension stores the following data locally on your device using Chrome's storage APIs:

  • Order Information: Transaction data including dates, amounts, payment details, nicknames, and related information that you manually register
  • Settings: Your preferences including backup folder settings and Banesco account credentials (stored locally, never transmitted)
  • Extracted Data: Temporary data extracted from web pages for form filling purposes

Data Collection Methods

  • Manual Entry: You manually enter and save order information through the Extension interface
  • Web Page Extraction: The Extension extracts data from web pages (specifically Binance C2C pages) when you use the extraction features
  • Form Filling: The Extension fills forms on web pages based on your saved data

How We Use Your Information

  • Order Management: Store and manage your transaction records locally
  • Form Filling: Automatically fill forms on web pages using your saved data
  • Data Analysis: Provide statistics and analysis of your orders (all calculations done locally)
  • Backup: Generate CSV backups of your data to your local Downloads folder

Data Storage and Security

  • Local Storage Only: All data is stored locally on your device using Chrome's IndexedDB and Chrome Storage APIs
  • No Cloud Sync: We do not transmit, sync, or store your data on external servers
  • No Third-Party Services: We do not share your data with third parties
  • Backup Files: CSV backup files are saved to your local Downloads folder - you control these files

Permissions Explained

  • storage: Required to save and retrieve your order data and settings locally
  • activeTab: Required to access the current tab for data extraction and form filling
  • scripting: Required to inject scripts for data extraction and form filling
  • tabs: Required to open new tabs for the Extension's pages (Ordenes, Analisis, Settings)
  • downloads: Required to save CSV backup files to your Downloads folder
  • host_permissions: Required to work on web pages (specifically Binance C2C pages)

Data Sharing

We do not share, sell, or transmit your data to any third parties. All data remains on your device.

Your Rights

  • Access: You can view all your stored data through the Extension's interface
  • Export: You can export your data as CSV files at any time
  • Delete: You can delete individual orders or all data through the Extension interface
  • Uninstall: Uninstalling the Extension will remove all stored data (you can export before uninstalling)

Children's Privacy

This Extension is not intended for users under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by updating the "Last Updated" date at the top of this policy.

Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us through the Chrome Web Store listing or your preferred method of contact.

Compliance

This Extension complies with:

  • Chrome Web Store Developer Program Policies
  • General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) principles
  • California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA) requirements

Note: This Extension operates entirely locally on your device. No data is transmitted to external servers.
